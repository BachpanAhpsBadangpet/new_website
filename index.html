Bachpan Global

Preschool Excellence (Playgroup - UKG)

India's leading preschool chain with 20+ years of experience in early childhood education. Our age-appropriate programs and unique concept rooms focus on developing cognitive, physical, social, emotional, and language abilities.

  • Play-way methods of learning
  • Unique concept rooms
  • Holistic development approach
  • Experienced educators

Academic Heights Public School

CBSE Board approved school established in 2009, known as one of the best K-12 schools in India. We offer ultra-modern infrastructure with smart classrooms, concept labs, and dedicated sports areas.
